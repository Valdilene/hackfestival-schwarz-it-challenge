# Chorizo Citrus Delight Pizza

## Ingredients:
* Black Mustard Seeds[2600.0]
* Chorizo Sausage Mild[3600.0]
* Cara Cara Oranges[2600.0]
* Yellow Bell Peppers[2600.0]
* Duck Eggs, 6 pcs[2600.0]
* Barley[2600.0]

## Steps:
* Prepare the barley according to package instructions and let it cool.
* Preheat the oven to 475°F (245°C).
* Roll out your pizza dough on a floured surface to your desired thickness.
* In a skillet, lightly toast the black mustard seeds until they begin to pop. Set aside.
* Cook the chorizo sausage in the skillet until it is browned and cooked through, then drain excess fat.
* Slice the yellow bell peppers into thin rings and set aside.
* Zest the Cara Cara oranges and then segment them, removing the membranes.
* Spread a thin layer of olive oil or tomato sauce on the pizza dough.
* Sprinkle the cooked barley evenly over the dough.
* Top with chorizo, yellow bell pepper rings, and segments of Cara Cara orange.
* Crack the duck eggs evenly across the pizza, being careful not to break the yolks.
* Sprinkle the toasted black mustard seeds on top for added crunch.
* Bake in the preheated oven for about 12-15 minutes, or until the crust is golden and the duck eggs are set to your liking.
* Remove from the oven and let it rest for a couple of minutes before slicing.
* Serve warm and enjoy your Chorizo Citrus Delight Pizza!

# Tropical Fiesta Pizza

## Ingredients:
* Chorizo Sausage Mild[3600.0]
* Kiwi[5200.0]
* Yellow Bell Peppers[3600.0]
* Duck Eggs, 6 pcs[4600.0]
* Barley[3600.0]

## Steps:
* Preheat the oven to 475°F (245°C).
* Roll out the pizza dough on a floured surface to your desired thickness.
* Spread a thin layer of olive oil on the pizza dough for extra flavor.
* Crumble the mild chorizo sausage and sauté it in a pan over medium heat until cooked through and slightly crispy.
* Slice the yellow bell peppers into thin strips and prepare them for topping.
* Slice the kiwi into thin rounds for a tropical twist.
* In a bowl, beat the duck eggs and season with salt and pepper.
* Spread the cooked chorizo evenly over the pizza base.
* Arrange the yellow bell pepper strips on top of the chorizo.
* Pour the beaten duck eggs over the toppings, ensuring an even distribution.
* Add the kiwi slices on top for a sweet contrast.
* Sprinkle precooked barley over the pizza for added texture.
* Bake in the preheated oven for 12-15 minutes or until the crust is golden and the eggs are set.
* Remove the pizza from the oven, let it cool slightly, slice, and serve.

# Citrus Duck Delight Pizza

## Ingredients:
* Duck Eggs, 6 pcs[7200.0]
* Nürnberger Sausage[4300.0]
* Cara Cara Oranges[7200.0]
* Brussels Sprouts[9000.0]
* Barley[7200.0]

## Steps:
* Preheat your oven to 475°F (245°C).
* Prepare the pizza dough on a baking tray or pizza stone.
* Chop the Nürnberger sausages into bite-sized pieces and sauté them in a skillet until browned.
* Trim and halve the Brussels sprouts, and blanch them in boiling water for 2-3 minutes, then shock them in ice water to keep their color.
* Zest one Cara Cara orange and set the zest aside. Then, slice the oranges into thin rounds.
* In a bowl, beat the duck eggs and season with salt and pepper.
* Spread the sautéed Nürnberger sausage evenly over the pizza dough.
* Add the blanched Brussels sprouts in a scattered manner across the pizza.
* Pour the beaten duck eggs over the top of the sausage and Brussels sprouts, allowing it to spread.
* Arrange the Cara Cara orange slices on top for a vibrant touch.
* Sprinkle the orange zest over the pizza for added citrus flavor.
* Bake in the oven for 12-15 minutes until the crust is golden and the egg is set.
* Let cool for a couple of minutes before slicing and serving.

# Citrus Barley Bliss Pizza

## Ingredients:
* Barley[29000.0]
* Cara Cara Oranges[22100.0]

## Steps:
* Preheat your oven to 475°F (245°C).
* In a bowl, combine 1 cup of barley with 2 cups of water, bring to a boil, then reduce to a simmer and cook for about 30-40 minutes until tender.
* While the barley is cooking, prepare a dough using 2 cups of flour, 1 teaspoon of yeast, and water as needed to form a workable dough.
* Knead the dough for about 5-7 minutes, then let it rise in a warm place for 30 minutes.
* Once the barley is cooked, drain any excess water and set aside.
* Zest one Cara Cara orange and squeeze juice from two oranges.
* In a bowl, mix the cooked barley with the orange zest, juice, a pinch of salt, and some olive oil.
* Roll out the pizza dough on a floured surface into your desired shape.
* Spread the barley mixture evenly over the pizza base.
* Optional: add cheese of your choice on top (like mozzarella or goat cheese).
* Bake the pizza in the preheated oven for about 10-12 minutes until the crust is golden.
* Remove from the oven, let cool slightly, and garnish with fresh herbs if desired.
* Slice, serve, and enjoy your Citrus Barley Bliss Pizza!
